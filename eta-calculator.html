    The math, out in the open

    No black box. This is the same arithmetic a good dispatcher runs in their head — just faster and without the 2am mistakes.

    Route miles

    We pull real road miles from OpenStreetMap routing. If the lookup can't find your points, type the miles in yourself and the rest still works.

    Honest drive time

    Miles ÷ your real average speed — fuel stops, scale houses, and city crawl included in that 55, not ignored like a car GPS does.
