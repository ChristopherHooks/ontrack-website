The money doesn't leak on the big stuff. It leaks load by load.

Detention you never billed. A broker you didn't screen. A truck that sat all day with no next load lined up. If your operation lives in your head and a notepad, you're paying for it every week — you just can't see where.

Unbilled detention

No in/out times logged means no detention invoiced. Two hours at a dock, twice a week, adds up to real money you already earned and never collected.

Unscreened brokers

One net-90 broker or one double-brokered load costs more than a year of the right tools. Vetting takes 60 seconds when you have a system for it.

No real numbers

Gross feels fine until you run the rate-per-mile. If you don't know your break-even before the broker names a number, you're negotiating blind.
